Serpent Stew

Description

Getting the meat for this stew is an arduous task, and requires a viking with combat prowess and good sea legs. In the end you'll make one of the heartiest meals an adventurer could ask for.

Ingredients

  • 1 Mushroom
  • 1 Cooked serpent meat
  • 2 Honey

Steps

  1. Begin by cooking your serpent meat over an open flame using your iron cooking station
  2. Move over to your cauldron and bring about a half cauldron of water to a boil
  3. Add your honey and mushroom to form your broth
  4. Then lower your meat into the broth and let it fall off the bone
  5. Leave it to cool for awhile before enjoying
