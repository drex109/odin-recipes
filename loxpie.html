Lox Pie

Description

This is a favorite of vikings who have braved the plains. A delicious meat pie with hints of sweetness and a golden brown crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 Lox meat
  • 2 Cloudberries
  • 4 Barley flour

Steps

  1. Take your ingredients to the food prep table and assemble your pie
  2. Carry your unbaked lox pie to the stone oven
  3. Light your oven with sufficient coal
  4. Leave pie in for 50-80 seconds
  5. Your oven should hold up to four pies at once
  6. Enjoy!
