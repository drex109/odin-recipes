Queen's Jam

Description

A simple jam for those vikings that have only just ventured into the Black Forest. A sweet treat perfect for eating by the spoonful!

Ingredients

  • 8 Raspberries
  • 6 Blueberries

Steps

  1. Gather your berries and take them to your cauldron
  2. Bring a quarter cauldron of water to a boil
  3. Drop in your berries and mash them into the water until the jam starts to form
  4. Pour into a beech wood jar and leave to cool
  5. Grab a spoon and enjoy!
